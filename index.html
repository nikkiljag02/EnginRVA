Empowering Ukrainian Youth Through English Mentorship

ENGinRVA connects Richmond volunteers with young Ukrainian learners for weekly English practice, personal growth, and cross-cultural community building.

Learn More

Our Mission

ENGinRVA fosters communication, confidence, and global connection by linking local mentors with Ukrainian students. Each relationship is tailored to the student’s goals, interests, and dreams. Our mission is to empower through language and create lifelong human connections.

Programs & How to Join

  • Volunteer Mentorship: Apply, get matched, and hold weekly video sessions with your student.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Volunteers and students choose the best times that fit their week.
  • Optional Events: Group sessions, cultural workshops, and bonding activities through ENGin Global.
