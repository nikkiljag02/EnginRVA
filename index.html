About Us

ENGin RVA is a chapter of the national ENGin organization based at Virginia Commonwealth University. We partner VCU students with Ukrainian students to practice English and build meaningful cross-cultural friendships.

Our Mission

Our goal is to support Ukrainian youth whose education has been disrupted by war. We aim to restore their academic confidence and give them a window into the world through English practice and peer mentorship.
